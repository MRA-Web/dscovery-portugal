Benefits of Obtaining the D7 Visa in Portugal


Legal Residence


The D7 visa allows retirees and individuals with their own income to legally reside in Portugal, providing a pathway to permanent residence and Portuguese citizenship.


Access to Health Services


With the D7 visa, residents have access to Portugal's public healthcare system, known for its quality and accessibility, ensuring excellent medical care.


Quality of Life


Portugal is known for its high quality of life, safety, pleasant climate, and relatively low cost of living. Obtaining the D7 visa allows you to enjoy these benefits in a welcoming country.


Traveling in Europe


The D7 visa allows free movement within the Schengen Area countries, facilitating travel and business within Europe without the need for additional visas.

How to Obtain the D7 Visa

Document Preparation

Gather documents such as proof of stable income, valid health insurance, and passport.

Visa Application

Submit your application to the Portuguese consulate or online, fill out the form, and schedule an interview if necessary.

Evaluation and Processing

Await evaluation by Portugal's SEF, which will verify document compliance and applicant eligibility.

Visa Issuance

Upon approval, obtain your D7 visa and fulfill additional formalities when moving to Portugal.
