Benefits of Obtaining the D7 Visa in Portugal

The D7 visa allows retirees and individuals with their own income to legally reside in Portugal, offering a path to permanent residence and Portuguese citizenship.


Access to Health Services


With the D7 visa, residents have access to Portugal's public health system, renowned for its quality and accessibility, ensuring quality medical care.


Quality of Life


Portugal is known for its high quality of life, safety, pleasant climate, and relatively low cost of living. Obtaining the D7 visa allows you to enjoy these benefits in a welcoming country.


Travel within Europe


The D7 visa allows free movement within the Schengen area countries, facilitating travel and business in Europe without the need for additional visas.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to obtain the D7 visa? >
The processing time can vary, but it generally takes between 60 to 90 days from the time of application submission.
Does the D7 visa allow family reunification?>
Yes, the D7 visa allows for family reunification. This means you can bring family members such as your spouse, dependent children, and even dependent parents to live with you in Portugal. Each family member must also meet certain requirements and obtain their own residence permit.
What is the validity of the D7 visa?>
The D7 visa is initially valid for 4 months, during which time you must apply for a residence permit. The residence permit is generally valid for 1 year and can be renewed.
Can I study in Portugal with the D7 visa?>
Yes, with the D7 visa you can study in Portugal. D7 visa holders have access to the Portuguese educational system, including public and private schools, universities, and other educational institutions.
